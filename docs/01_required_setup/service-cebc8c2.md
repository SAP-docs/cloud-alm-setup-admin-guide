# Service

In the following sections, you can find detailed breakdowns of all roles related to SAP Cloud ALM for service, mapped to the individual actions they allow users to perform.





### Disclaimer

SAP makes no warranty, either expressed or implied, for the information provided here. Due to short-term software updates, completeness cannot be guaranteed and the information is subject to change. For the same reasons, translations of this guide into languages other than English may not reflect the latest changes.



### Interpreting the Tables

-   **Yes**: The action is available to users with this role.

-   **No**: The action is not available to users with this role.


> ### Tip:  
> To find out which apps are available to each role, search for **Access the app** in the **Action** column.

## Service Collaboration

> ### Note:  
> Each role for SAP Cloud ALM for service must be combined with a role from *Project Management*:
> 
> -   *Services Administrator* with *Project Administrator*
> 
> -   *Services Expert* with *Project Member*
> 
> -   *Services Viewer* with *Project Viewer*
> 
> 
> Therefore, also consider the permissions granted by the *Project Management* roles, as listed under [Project Management](implementation-782a563.md#loio782a5636ae874e53830d5198911bf794__section_tll_s4c_y2c).
